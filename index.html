Biography

Welcome! I‚Äôm Dr. Nazir Ahmed Loan, Assistant Professor (C) in the Department of Electronics & IT, University of Kashmir. My work focuses on multimedia security, multimedia watermarking and copyright protection, digital image processing, computer vision, deepfake detection, and intelligent embedded systems. I am a Gold Medalist (M.Sc. Electronics) and a recipient of the prestigious DST-INSPIRE JRF/SRF Fellowship awarded by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. I hold a Ph.D. in Electronics, with research contributions published in leading journals including IEEE Access, IEEE IoT Journal, Future Generation Computer Systems, and Nonlinear Dynamics.
I teach and mentor postgraduate students in microcontrollers, embedded programming, digital image processing, signals & systems, computer architecture, and data structures. I also teach a wide range of programming languages and tools, including C, C++, Embedded C, Python, MATLAB, Arduino programming, and Assembly language. My broader interests include secure e-healthcare systems, smart IoT solutions, and the development of AI-based multimedia analysis frameworks.
Thank you for visiting! Feel free to explore my research, projects, publications, and teaching materials.
